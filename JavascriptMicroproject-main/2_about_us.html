Our Founder - Mr.Raju Kumar

  • The Green Leaf is a chain of luxury restaurants and a subsidiary of The Green Leaf Company Limited, headquartered in Mumbai, India.
  • Founded by Raju Kumar in January 2001, the company is part of The Green Leaf Group, one of India's largest catering conglomerates.
  • In 2022, the company employed over 200 people and operates over 15 restaurants and hotel-resorts, with 84 locations in Maharashtra and 6 in other states like Bengal, Delhi, Chennai, and Bangalore.
  • Raju Kumar opened the first Green Leaf hotel and restaurant in Mumbai (Bandra) on 2nd January 2022, overlooking the Arabian Sea.
  • Anecdotal stories suggest the hotel was opened after Raju Kumar was refused entry to the Watson Hotel in Mumbai due to past business failures, leading him to accept a challenge from a friend and business partner.


Our Head chef - Mr.Sanjeev Kapoor

  • Chef Sanjeev Kapoor is the head chef of The Green Leaf, which has 16 restaurants in India.
  • At 55 years old, he is a renowned chef, entrepreneur, and TV personality, famous for his popular cookery.
  • He joined The Green Leaf Group in February 2022, becoming India's first chef to lead a 16-restaurant chain.
  • Recognized in Reader's Digest as one of 100 of India's Most Trusted People.
  • Known for developing the indulgent Indian menu featuring lamb kebabs and signature gravies as specialties.
  • Played a key role in developing the Continental and Italian menus, making him an integral part of the restaurant chain.



Our Specialities

• Lamb Kebabs -
The Doner Kebab is also called a lambkebab. Many believe that this brown coloured, spit-roasted, thinly sliced meat, is primarily made from processed lamb meat with some seasoning. However, the meat used for making doner kebab meat may be lamb,veal or mutton but not pork. The lamb Kebabs are the most indlgent and flavourful of our dishes since the restaurant opened in February, it has remained the the second most ordered item on our menu after our Italian cuisine.

• Sundried Tomato and Arugula Pizza -
Peppery greens tossed in lemon juice are a fresh final topping for this sun dried tomato arugula pizza. Those peppery leaves quite take this arugula pizza with sun dried tomatoes into full taste They just layer in another something to the usual pizza components including crust, tomatoes and cheese. The pizza is tossed in olive oil and grilled to perfection in a wood fire oven which is rarely used in India. The simplest way to get quality crust is with the heat of the grill. Putting the dough right on the grates gives grill marks and that nice smoky flavor.All these factors have made this decadent Italian dish into the most ordered food item in our restaurant chain.

• Osso Bucco -
Ossobuco or osso buco is a specialty of Lombard cuisine of cross-cut veal shanks braised with vegetables, white wine and broth. It is often garnished with gremolata and traditionally served with either risotto alla milanese or polenta, depending on the regional variation. The marrow in the hole in the bone, a prized delicacy, is the defining feature of the dish. The two types of ossobuco are a modern version that has tomatoes and the original version which does not. The older version, ossobuco in bianco, is flavoured with cinnamon, bay leaf, and gremolata. The modern and more popular recipe includes tomatoes, carrots, celery and onions; gremolata is optional.
